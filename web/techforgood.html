Cancer Screening

As part of Grace Hopper Conference, in association with Cancer Society of India,we developed an app to help in the process of screening for throat cancer. The process in place lacked maintiance of records and it was difficult to track a patient once screened. Using the app developed by us, it is possible to track the patients, assign doctors and follow up with the patient for the entire duration. A rating system was developed to help the volunteers assign good doctors based on previous feedback. Offline access for the app was provided. Charts could be generated for better reporting. The was developed using ionic and cordova with a mongo db Backend.

Website for the Physically Disabled

As part of a 24 hour hackathon at J P Morgan India, in association with Enable India, I helped develop a website for the physically disabled to create a mentor mentee relatioship between the commuity. This would be useful in searching for jobs as well developing skills of choice. The website was developed keeping in mind the visually impaored as well. It was readble thorugh JAWS as the tags structure was maintained as required by JAWS. The color schemes was suited for those with limtd vision. My team won the first place in the NGO round for this project.

Virtual Police Station

As part of The Great Mind Challenege, in a team of 3, we developed a virtual police station. Through this, citizens could file complaints and police could resolve cases or request more information without having to be physically present. This saved time and resources and makes the law and order community easily reachable. We provided services for reporting of thefts and petty crimes. We qualified to the last round of the competition.

Drink Safe

Drink Safe is a phone application that ensures the safety of the people who are under the influence of alcohol. The phone judges how intoxicated a person is using the accelerometer and GPS. The way the person walks, the location of the person and other factors were used to create an app that learns and differentiates between the normal and drunk state of person. This hack was submitted in Ayana 2013.
